# PCG-Enhancement

Concept:

Library of the Magus
The player is Aldric, a young scholar who inherits a rusted key and a cryptic note from his late grandfather, the reclusive mage Erasmus — "See you in my quarters." The key unlocks the entrance to his grandfather's long-sealed tower, a vast labyrinth of interconnected library chambers, each containing two ancient portals that transport the player to a random room deeper in the tower. Aldric must explore the chambers, collect scattered spell-books and journal fragments that reveal his grandfather's secrets, and navigate the unpredictable portal network to reach the personal quarters hidden at the heart of the tower.
